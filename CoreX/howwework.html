Introducing How We Work

OUR APPROACH

Our objective consists in defeating problems related to self-recognization, by bringing out your hidden treasures in front of you. In order to develop a self-image you are proud of, we organize our approach around three pillars.

1 • Refining your career-objective

Your objectives are closely related to the specificities of your industry and your organization. In order to nurture a relavent talent, we start by closely analyzing your mission, your organization and your needs. Along this phase we work closely with you on the creation process, so that the project progresses in accordance with your objective and the way you want to work.

2 • Understanding your potential

Our purpose is to analyze and understand the value you bring to tahe table. From the beginning to the end of a project, we work extensively with the you, so that your better self can be reflected in a better way, through our visualization.

3 • Serving your inner-self

We strongly believe that easy-to-understand humans are the ones that can adapt to different sceneriaos and their own needs. To make something out of yourself, we carefully analyze the training needed, so you can be empowered, as they make your talents, your own.

OUR METHODOLOGY

We have developed a consistent working process revolving around three phases, in order to effectively translate raw talent to one-of-its-kind skill

Phase 1: Immersion

Objective: This phase allows us to precisely define the expectations, technical constraints, as well as who the audience types are and what their expectations are. During this phase, we also deeply analyze the talents, in order to fully understand the value brought to the company.

Result: At the end of this phase, we can suggest scenarios of work and satisfaction that can aptly meet the expressed needs. Those scenarios are then debated across the next steps of the development. We work alongside our end users to assist with those decisions.

Phase 2: Conception

Objective: This phase allows us to translate into real worls, the use of your so found talents, and into rules to manage your being. We determine how all the pieces should be put together, and implemented within their domain of application. We then specify the training processes as well as the technical choices.

Result: According to each particular project this phase can lead to a prototype of your full potential.

Phase 3: Development

Objective: During the production phase, mamangers and leaders within our team work closely to knit you together.

Result: You are now ready to face your new life... Bon voyage!
